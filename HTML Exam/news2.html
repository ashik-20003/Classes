Ram and SSD prices hike.

The technology market has witnessed a significant surge in RAM and SSD prices in recent months, affecting consumers and businesses alike. Industry analysts attribute this price increase to supply chain disruptions and increased demand for high-performance computing components. Manufacturers report that DDR5 RAM and NVMe SSDs have seen the steepest price hikes, with some models experiencing increases of up to 20%. Market experts predict that prices may stabilize in the coming quarters as production capacity increases and supply chain issues resolve.
